Q: What is a text message (SMS)?

SMS stands for Short Message Service, and it is synonymous with text message, text, TXT, etc. An SMS (text message) is a short message, which can be sent to and from a cellular phone.

Q: Can my phone receive text messages?

Nearly all phones purchased in the last ten years are capable of sending and receiving text messages.

Q: What is the advantage of using text messaging?

Text messages are a brief, effective, inexpensive means of communication. Most people carry their cellular phones with them wherever they go, so unlike email, text messages can reach people when they are away from their desks.

Q: Can you guarantee delivery of every text message that I send?

No. As text messaging is a store-and-forward technology that is dependent on carriers and their networks, it is impossible to guarantee delivery of a text message.

Q: Can I change the number or set a name from which you send my text messages?

Unfortunately, it isn't possible to send a message from a name as phone calls and text messages always come from phone numbers. There are two different kinds of phone numbers:  regular ten-digit numbers like the one you have on your cell phone, and short coded, which are special five- or six-digit numbers like ours. For a message to appear from a name it must be stored in your phone’s address book. You will notice when you receive a call or text message from a person who is not in your phone book, it appears as their ten-digit number (not a name), since it is not stored in your phone. The same applies to a short code.

Q: How much do text messages cost to send using EZ Texting?

Text messages cost 5 cents per message (7 cents in Canada). If sending 1,000 text messages, the cost is $50 ($70 in Canada) on the Pay & Go plan. We do not charge for Incoming messages. Please see our Pricing page for complete details.

Q: Do you charge any fees? Do I need to sign a monthly contract?

EZ Texting will never charge you any fees, or require you to sign a contract. When you open an EZ Texting account it is set to the Pay & Go plan. Once you use up the free credits in your account, you can purchase credits to pay for the texts you wish to send.

In addition to our Pay & Go plan, we offer monthly plans, which include discounted messages and Keywords. Please see our Pricing page for complete details.

Q: How long can I save my purchased Credits?

Unused purchased credits expire 18 months after their purchase date. If you are on a monthly plan, your credits reset at the end of each billing period.

Q: Do you charge for incoming text messages?

No, we do not charge you for incoming text messages.

Q: Does my carrier (or my contact's carrier) charge for received messages?

Every carrier offers different text messaging packages.

Q: Can you forward incoming texts to my email address for free?

Yes! Unlike many of our competitors, we do not charge you anything to forward your incoming text messages to your email address.

Q: What about to my personal mobile phone?

Forwarded messages to your cellular phone cost one credit per message forwarded.

Q: Can I use my EZ Texting account to send group/bulk text messages?

Yes! That is exactly what your EZ Texting account is for. We allow you to create and sort your contacts into lists in order to send group/bulk messages with the click of a button.

Q: Can I receive replies to the group text messages that I send?

Yes. They will be delivered to your EZ Texting inbox as long as the reply is received within twelve hours of your original text. If you'd like, you can have us forward your replies to your cell phone or your email address.
